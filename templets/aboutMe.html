h2

how I start

/h2 p

My web development journey began two years ago when I was first introduced to HTML in college, and I wasn't much interested in developing something from it. In my final year, I was required to take an industrial training course.
During this training, we were told to join an IT company and study how it works. As I know html from my college, I chose to gain knowledge about web development at Ekatta Innovators, as they picked me up as a full-time employee as soon as we started training.
After that, I started working on their live projects where someone who has no background in web development was assigned to build websites for clients. Initially I was scared but then I asked my mentors Shubam Sir and Jayant Sir, who helped me to gain the skills needed to develop websites.
Onwords, I began exploring various frameworks like Bootstrap for rapid responsive website development. Over the course of a month, I learned the basics of Dom. later I encountered DJnago and started studying it.
About a month later, I made my first web app using DJnago. From then on, I had to learn on my own. Now, I've completed over 10 static sites and 5 web apps.
